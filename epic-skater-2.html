Epic Skater 2 – Master Your Skateboard Combos

Epic Skater 2 is a fast-paced online skateboarding game where players chain together complex tricks across the bustling city of Epicwood. The game emphasizes skillful combo execution, exploration, and continuous progression for anyone aiming to dominate the leaderboards.

Customize Your Skater

Before hitting the streets, players can design their ideal skater using the extensive Create-A-Skater system. With over 600 clothing items and accessories, the combinations are nearly limitless. Completing daily challenges unlocks exclusive gear, allowing each player to express their own style while boosting performance.

Explore Epicwood and Upgrade Tricks

Epicwood encourages vertical and horizontal exploration, from street-level ramps to rooftop half-pipes and subway tunnels. Over 44 upgradeable tricks are available, including flips, grabs, grinds, acid drops, spine transfers, wallplants, and extended grabs. Strategically chaining these maneuvers not only maximizes your score but also uncovers hidden areas and boost pads to maintain speed during long combos.

Gameplay Modes

Career Mode challenges players through 40 levels with objectives that test timing, precision, and combo mastery.

Endless Mode removes level limits and focuses on sustained performance. It includes 11 progression tiers and over 1,600 goals, ideal for breaking distance records or perfecting extended trick sequences.

Game Controls

PC: Arrow keys or movement keys for direction; mouse click/hold for tricks and grabs.

Mobile/Tablet: Swipe to move, touch-and-hold for grabs, and on-screen buttons for boost pads and other actions.

Maintaining speed and precision is essential to keeping combo chains alive and scoring high.

Tips for High Scores

Focus on linking tricks smoothly rather than rushing. Observe the environment to take advantage of boost pads and hidden paths. Experiment with different trick sequences to find the most rewarding combos, and balance risk with control to push your scoring potential.
