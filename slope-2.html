Gameplay Tips

  • Look Ahead: Anticipate twists and gaps rather than focusing only on the ball.
  • Stay Centered: Middle of the track gives more room to adjust.
  • Small Movements: Oversteering can quickly lead to failure.
  • Practice Regularly: Each attempt improves reflexes and understanding of the slope patterns.
